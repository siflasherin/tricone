Building Maintenance:


Building maintenance works ensure the safety, functionality, and aesthetics of a structure over time. Here’s a comprehensive list of typical building maintenance activities:

Structural Maintenance

  • Concrete repairs
  • Brickwork repairs
  • Roof repairs
  • Facade cleaning

Electrical Maintenance

  • Wiring and cabling
  • Lighting repairs and replacements
  • Power supply maintenance
  • Generator servicing

Plumbing Maintenance

  • Leak repairs
  • Pipe replacements
  • Drain cleaning
  • Water heater servicing

HVAC Maintenance

  • Air filter replacement
  • Duct cleaning
  • AC servicing
  • Heating system maintenance

Painting and Decoration

  • Interior painting
  • Exterior painting
  • Wallpaper installation
  • Surface preparation

Fire Safety Maintenance

  • Fire alarm testing
  • Sprinkler system maintenance
  • Fire extinguisher checks
  • Emergency lighting maintenance

Grounds Maintenance

  • Landscaping
  • Irrigation system
These maintenance activities help prolong the lifespan of a building, ensure the safety of its occupants, and maintain its value.
