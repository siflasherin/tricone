MEP:


MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) works are essential for the functionality and safety of buildings. Here’s a comprehensive list of MEP works:

Mechanical Works

  • HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning)
  • Fire Protection Systems
  • Pumps and Elevators
  • Refrigeration Systems

Electrical Works

  • Power Distribution
  • Lighting Systems
  • Electrical Fixtures and Outlets
  • Building Management Systems (BMS)
  • Communication Systems

Plumbing Works

  • Water Supply Systems
  • Drainage Systems
  • Sanitary Systems
  • Backflow Prevention
  • Water Conservation Systems

Integration and Coordination

  • MEP Coordination
  • Testing and Commissioning
These MEP works are crucial for ensuring that a building operates efficiently, safely, and comfortably for its occupants.
